# A Credible Website for a Specialist Consultancy

ECOS Consulting needed a professional website that could carry the trust expected in safety, health, and environmental consulting. The site had to explain their services clearly, support procurement conversations, and stay easy for the team to update.

## The Brief

For a B2B consulting firm, the website is often part of the credibility check. Prospective clients need to understand the service scope, see a professional standard, and feel confident making contact.

## What We Delivered

We designed and built a fast, structured website with clear service pages, credibility-focused content, SEO foundations, and a CMS workflow for ongoing updates.

The visual direction is clean and professional, with an emphasis on clarity, trust, and easy navigation across the firm’s service areas.

## Why It Works

The site gives ECOS Consulting a stronger business presence without creating ongoing technical overhead. The team can keep content current, while prospective clients get a clearer view of the firm’s expertise and services.

## Outcome

ECOS Consulting now has a professional web presence that supports search visibility, business development, and client confidence.
