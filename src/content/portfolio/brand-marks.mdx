This collection brings together logo and identity work created for businesses across hospitality, healthcare, technology, events, retail, and professional services.

## The Brief

Each business needed a clear visual anchor that could work beyond a single logo file. These marks had to show up consistently across websites, signage, stationery, social profiles, uniforms, packaging, presentations, and customer-facing materials.

## What We Delivered

We developed marks that balance recognition, clarity, and flexibility. Some are quiet and professional. Others are more expressive. In each case, the design direction was shaped around the business category, audience, and practical places the brand needed to appear.

## Why It Works

A strong mark gives a business something to build around. It helps customers recognise the brand faster and gives teams a consistent foundation for future design work.

## Outcome

The result is a broad identity showcase that demonstrates how Lumina7 approaches logo design as part of a usable brand system, not as an isolated graphic.
