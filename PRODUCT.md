# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

South African owner-run businesses, growing SMEs, and ambitious founders who need credible brand, print, signage, and web work without long agency timelines. Visitors are typically comparing providers, looking for proof of quality, and deciding whether Lumina7 feels practical, trustworthy, and easy to work with.

## Product Purpose

Lumina7's website showcases the studio's work, explains its services clearly, and turns qualified interest into direct enquiries. Success means prospects quickly understand what the studio offers, see relevant proof, trust the delivery process, and feel confident starting a conversation.

## Brand Personality

Confident, practical, and thoughtful. Professional without feeling corporate, creative without being flashy, and outcome-oriented without relying on agency jargon.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic creative-agency spectacle, inflated innovation language, anonymous template layouts, decorative effects that compete with the work, and portfolio copy written like an individual job application. The site should not feel overly academic, overly luxurious, or trend-led at the expense of clarity.

## Design Principles

1. Let real work carry the visual authority.
2. Make services, pricing signals, timelines, and next steps easy to understand.
3. Use distinctive craft with restraint so the studio feels capable rather than performative.
4. Speak in practical business outcomes and plain language.
5. Keep the experience fast, responsive, and consistent across light and dark themes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain WCAG AA contrast, semantic structure, keyboard-visible focus states, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion support. Do not rely on colour alone to communicate state or meaning.
